# nutriai
NutriAI is an AI-powered nutrition tracking app that goes far beyond counting calories. While most apps only track basic macros, NutriAI gives you a complete picture of everything your body is getting — down to every amino acid, fatty acid, vitamin, and mineral.
Log your meals by searching our library of 300+ foods, scanning a barcode, or simply taking a photo and letting the AI estimate your nutrition automatically. Get personalized AI-generated meal plans optimized for your exact goals, dietary restrictions, and micronutrient needs. Track your water intake, weight progress, supplements, and fitness activity all in one place.
NutriAI also learns your patterns over time — alerting you when you're consistently missing key nutrients and suggesting specific foods to fix the gaps before they affect your health.
Built for everyone from serious athletes to people just starting their health journey, NutriAI makes professional-level nutrition tracking simple, smart, and accessible.
